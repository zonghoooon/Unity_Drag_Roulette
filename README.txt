This is roullette game!😎

You can rotate the roulette by drag👆

Rotation speed increases with drag distance📈 and decreases with drag time📉.
Roulette automatically stop at the correct position below a certain speed!

if you click the ➕ button items in roullete is added.
if you click the ➖ button items in roullete is removed.
Items in roullete automatically find their location!
Items can be from 2 to 7. 🔴🟠🟡🟢🔵🟣🟤
